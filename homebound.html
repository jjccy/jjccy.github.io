Project overview

Project Description

"Homebound" is a two month team project with Unity from class IAT 410: Advance Game Design. The goal of this project is to create a intersting game with the limited time period we have. "Homebound" An Isometric exploratory roguelike game with crafting elements. It uses a comedic tone as the premise involves catching different creatures and putting them together to build a spaceship to get home. We hope to create an exciting game that has a unique mechanism with the limited time we have. I hope everyone will enjoy this game.

Process

Motivation

From the beginning, our group want to create something related to spaceships and aliens. After a quick brainstorm, I come up with the idea of creating a spaceship with alien animals to escape the planet. Base upon this concept, we come up with some interesting creatures with their unique ability that may be useful for building a spaceship.

Cut out mechanisms

You could tell from the video in our original design, player could visit outside of the nest. However, this is soon removed after this video because it gives the player too much freedom instead of experience the story. Original the game also has day and night, the entire story happens across couple days in-game time, but we realize that it will cause the story become too lengthy, players will get bored before the end of the story.

Due to time constrain, we donâ€™t have too much time to work on this project. Therefore we end up cutting off a lot of cool ideas in our minds. In our original design, after the player finishes building the spaceship, the player needs to fly through a wormhole in the air manually to escape the planet. We also planted to implement 12 different creatures, and each has its own characteristic. The player only needs to collect some to build a functional spaceship. These ideas have to go because they are just not feasible in the time we have. Other ideas that left behind includes but not limited to:

  • A more flexible spaceship building interface
  • Creating multiple ways to build a spaceship
  • Add collectables to catch creatures
  • Some collectables and animals could boost player abilities
  • A more dynamic environment design

There are many more fun ideas like this, but one of the most interesting ideas was inspired by how birds and humans see colour differently. We were thinking to change all colour in the game world to a different colour palate to "mimic" how birds see the world. In that case, players won't tell the difference between cigarettes and worm, other than its movement.

This idea finally got redesigned because it is too aline like, which will break the immersive. However, it helps us to re-think our approach and lead us to the current way to create models for food and garbage.
